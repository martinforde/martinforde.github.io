King's College London

Dr Martin Forde

Financial Mathematics

martin.forde@kcl.ac.uk

Rough volatility, Sinkhorn calibration, Entropic optimal transport, VIX options, Transient Price Impact

  • Solving the FX cross-smiles problem - rate of convergence for Sinkhorn marginals, and the finite-option case (updated)
  • Rough Bergomi revisited - exact and minimal-variance hedging for VIX and European options, and exact calibration to multiple smiles
  • Lvy processes as weak limits of rough Heston models, with A.Bondi
  • Small-time VIX smile and the stationary distribution for the Rough Heston model, with S.Gerhold and B.Smith
  • Rough Heston with jumps - joint calibration to SPX/VIX level and skew as T0, and issues with the quadratic rough Heston model, with B.Smith
  • Optimality properties of Dupire and Brunick-Shreve diffusion-type processes - extremal prices of vol swaps and VIX futures given full marginals
