Biography

Martin Forde is a lecturer in financial mathematics in the Department of Mathematics at King’s College London. Before joining King’s he held a research fellowship at Dublin City University and was previously a visiting assistant professor at the University of California, Santa Barbara.

Research interests

  • Rough volatility
  • Price impact models
  • Gaussian fields and multiplicative chaos
  • Robust hedging of exotic options
